About

April 18, 2026 · 1 min · Nidhi Singh

I am an independent ML researcher based in Paderborn, Germany, currently pursuing an M.Sc. in AI at Universität zu Lübeck.

My research interest is in learning compact world representations through latent-space prediction — the JEPA lineage (LeCun et al.) — without pixel reconstruction, for sequential decision-making.

Current projects:

  • World Models (Ha & Schmidhuber) replication on CarRacing-v3 — identified MDN-RNN logvar collapse as a novel finding
  • I-JEPA partial replication (in progress)

This blog is my research reflection journal — paper notes, implementation findings, and open questions!
